Samagra & Aadhaar Linking

We assist schools and students in linking and correcting Samagra & Aadhaar data:

  • Samagra family ID creation & update
  • Linking student data with Aadhaar
  • Correction in name, date of birth, or other details
  • Submission of linking status to portals

Documents Required for Samagra & Aadhaar Services:

  • Student Aadhar Card
  • Samagra ID (Family & Individual)
  • Birth Certificate
  • Ration Card / Electricity Bill (for address proof)
  • Parent’s Aadhar Card
  • Photograph (Passport-sized)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • School ID Card (if available)

Please ensure all documents are clearly scanned and match official records.
