OASIS Portal Services

We provide expert support for data management on the OASIS portal:

  • Staff and school data entry & updates
  • Online submission and corrections
  • Uploading required documents for verification
  • Support for data lock and final submission

Documents Required for OASIS Portal Work:

  • School UDISE Code
  • Principal’s Aadhaar Card
  • Samagra ID (Staff/Students)
  • Staff Photograph (Passport-size)
  • Staff Aadhaar Card
  • Educational Qualification Certificates
  • Joining Letter/Appointment Order
  • Caste Certificate (for reserved category staff)
  • Income Certificate (if required)
  • Experience Certificate (if applicable)

All scanned documents must be clear and in acceptable formats (PDF/JPEG).
