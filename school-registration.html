School Registration

We provide complete support for new school registration and renewal processes:

  • Online registration for new schools
  • Renewal of existing registrations
  • Assistance in document preparation and uploads
  • Guidance through government portal processes

Documents Required for School Registration:

  • School Building Ownership/Lease Document
  • School Photograph (Building and Classrooms)
  • Principal’s Aadhar Card
  • Samagra ID
  • Recognition Certificate from Authority
  • Bank Passbook (in school’s name)
  • Affidavit (as per rules)
  • Fire Safety Certificate
  • Health and Sanitation Certificate
  • Society/Trust Registration Certificate
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-sized Photograph (Principal/Head)

Ensure all documents are valid, clear, and as per the guidelines before submission.
