Teacher Services

We provide full support for teacher-related services such as:

  • Joining and relieving process
  • Leave application and tracking
  • Profile updates (promotion, subject change, etc.)
  • Online record correction & approval support

Documents Required for Teacher Services:

  • Aadhar Card of Teacher
  • Joining Letter / Relieving Letter
  • Appointment Order
  • Qualification Certificates (10th, 12th, UG, PG, B.Ed, etc.)
  • Experience Certificate (if applicable)
  • Leave Application (in case of leave-related work)
  • Photograph (Passport-sized)
  • Samagra ID
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (if required for any claim)

Please provide scanned copies of original documents for faster processing.
