RTE Portal Management

We assist schools in managing all RTE-related processes:

  • Admission application entry
  • Document verification
  • Seat allotment and tracking
  • Application status monitoring

Documents Required for RTE Portal:

  • Birth Certificate of the Student
  • Aadhar Card of Student
  • Income Certificate of Parent(s)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Address Proof (Electricity/Water Bill, etc.)
  • Guardian's Aadhar (if applicable)
  • Recent Passport-sized Photograph of Student
  • Samagra ID

Please ensure that all documents are clear and legible to avoid any delays in processing.
