School Login & Staff Management

We help schools manage and resolve login access issues and staff data updates including:

  • School login creation and recovery
  • Staff profile creation and updates
  • Staff ID generation and verification
  • Login-related technical support

Documents Required for School Login & Staff Services:

  • School UDISE Code
  • Principal’s Aadhar Card
  • Official Email ID (if available)
  • Staff Aadhar Cards
  • Appointment Orders of Staff
  • Staff Qualification Certificates
  • Staff Photographs (Passport-sized)
  • Samagra ID (for each staff member)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (if required)

Please ensure all documents are up-to-date and clearly scanned for processing.
