Scholarship Assistance

We help students and schools with scholarship form filling, verification, and documentation including:

  • Online application form submission
  • Document upload & verification guidance
  • Tracking scholarship status
  • Correction of student details (if needed)

Documents Required for Scholarship Assistance:

  • Student Aadhar Card
  • Samagra ID
  • Caste Certificate (mandatory for reserved category)
  • Income Certificate (latest)
  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous Year Marksheet
  • Bank Passbook (student or parent)
  • Photograph (Passport-sized)
  • Domicile Certificate (if applicable)
  • Parent's Aadhar Card

Ensure all documents are scanned clearly and submitted within the portal deadline.
