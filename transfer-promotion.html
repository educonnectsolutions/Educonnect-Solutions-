Transfer & Promotion

We offer assistance in managing staff transfer and promotion cases including:

  • Online transfer applications
  • Promotion case documentation
  • Departmental approvals & follow-up
  • Record corrections & updates

Documents Required for Transfer & Promotion:

  • Application for Transfer / Promotion
  • Aadhar Card of Teacher/Staff
  • Joining Letter and Appointment Order
  • Last Transfer Order (if any)
  • Promotion Eligibility Certificate
  • Service Book Pages (relevant entries)
  • Samagra ID
  • Photograph (Passport-sized)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (if required)

Ensure all documents are self-attested and scanned clearly for smooth processing.
