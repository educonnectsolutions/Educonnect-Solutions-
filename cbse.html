CBSE Portal Assistance

We provide complete assistance for CBSE-related school work:

  • LOC (List of Candidates) submission for Class 10 & 12
  • Registration of new students
  • Subject correction and updates
  • Migration & Transfer case assistance
  • Board exam data upload and verification

Documents Required for CBSE Work:

  • Student Aadhar Card
  • Samagra ID
  • Previous Class Marksheet
  • Birth Certificate
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Photograph (Passport-sized)
  • Parent’s Aadhar Card
  • School ID Card (if available)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)

Note: Ensure all details are accurate and match school records before uploading to the portal.
