About Us

Educonnect Solutions is dedicated to providing trusted and reliable portal services for schools. We handle government-authorized tenders across the academic year and ensure all portal-related services are delivered efficiently and accurately. Our team is known for its timely and responsible execution of work assigned under official educational projects.

Every financial year, we take responsibility for official school-related tenders issued through the BRC (Block Resource Centre) offices. If your school falls under any such BRC jurisdiction, we ensure that all the required services for your school are handled professionally and completed before the deadline. Our commitment lies in delivering quality service with complete accountability.

We also assure the complete protection of student, parent, and staff data. At Educonnect Solutions, maintaining confidentiality and data privacy is a top priority�no sensitive information is ever disclosed or shared.
