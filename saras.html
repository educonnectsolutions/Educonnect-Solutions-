SARAS 6.0 Portal Assistance

We provide comprehensive assistance for managing school affiliations and updates on the SARAS 6.0 portal:

  • Online submission of school affiliation details
  • Assistance with registration, renewal, and revalidation
  • Document upload and verification support
  • Data correction and submission management
  • Help with submission of various reports and forms

Documents Required for SARAS 6.0 Portal:

  • School UDISE Code
  • School Building Ownership/Lease Agreement
  • Fire Safety Certificate
  • Health and Sanitation Certificate
  • Principal’s Aadhaar Card
  • Samagra ID
  • Photograph (School Building and Principal)
  • Recognition/Approval Certificate from the Government
  • Bank Passbook (School Account)
  • Society/Trust Registration Certificate
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)

Please ensure that all documents are valid and properly scanned before uploading them on the SARAS portal.
