Enrollment Management

We help schools manage student admissions and maintain accurate enrollment records:

  • New student entry & data upload
  • Class-wise enrollment updates
  • Correction of student details
  • Promotion & transfer data entries

Documents Required for Enrollment Management:

  • Student Aadhar Card
  • Student Birth Certificate
  • Previous Class Marksheet / TC (if applicable)
  • Photograph (Passport-sized)
  • Parent's Aadhar Card
  • Samagra ID
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • Bank Passbook (if linked for scholarship)

All documents should be scanned clearly and verified before submission.
